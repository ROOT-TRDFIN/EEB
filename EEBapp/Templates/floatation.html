Technical Details

I-Soul Pod, also known as a sensory deprivation tank, is a specialized device or a small, enclosed pool designed to create a unique sensory experience. The concept behind a floatation tank is to provide a space where an individual can float effortlessly in a dense solution of water and Epsom salts, creating a sensation of weightlessness. It is mainly used for controlled constrained Stimulation therapy.

It is made so dark and soundproof that we need to control the influence of external stimuli on the subject body responses while, so he/she is floating in an Epsom salt solution maintained at an optimum body temperature. This enables the subject to enter a deep meditative state, while so relaxing the entire physical system and completely losing awareness of all stimuli, causing a feeling of rejuvenation after a complete floating session of about an average of sixty minutes approximately.

Floatation tanks are primarily used for relaxation, stress reduction, and mental well-being. The experience of floating in the tank can help promote deep relaxation, alleviate stress and anxiety, reduce muscle tension, and improve sleep quality. Many people also report enhanced creativity, mental clarity, and a sense of rejuvenation after.
